Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Severe Techno, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to improve your experience and ensure the functionality of our services:

  • Personal Information: Data like name, email address, and phone number, only if you choose to provide it for feedback or support.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our app, such as translation queries, error reports, and device information.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is utilized for:

  • Providing and maintaining our services.
  • Improving the quality and functionality of the translator.
  • Addressing user queries and feedback.
  • Enhancing security and detecting fraudulent activities.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

Severe Techno employs advanced security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission.
  • Regular security audits to ensure our systems are up-to-date.
  • Limited access to your data, restricted to authorized personnel only.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we have about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data collection (where applicable).

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools and services, such as analytics platforms, to improve the app. These services have their own privacy policies, which we recommend reviewing.

Changes to This Policy

Our Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be communicated via:

  • Notifications on the app.
  • Updates on our official website.
  • Updates on our social media pages.

Security Principles

Data Encryption

All sensitive data transmitted through our services is encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols to ensure data security during transit.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and address potential risks.

Access Control

Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel only. We ensure strict role-based access control to safeguard user information.

Incident Management

Severe Techno has an established incident response plan to handle security breaches promptly and effectively, minimizing the impact on users.
